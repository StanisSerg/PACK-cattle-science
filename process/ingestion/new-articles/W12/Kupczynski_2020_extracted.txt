changesoccuringincattle. Dietaryglycerolisbeneficialtothedairycowbecauseitappearstoincrease
ruminalpropionate,therebyincreasingthesupplyofgluconeogenicsubstratetotheliver. However,as
thedoseofglycerolincreases,theabundanceofButyrivibrioFibrisolvensandSelenomonasruminantium
intherumendecreases. Usedasapartialreplacementforgrain,glycerolhasahighcalorificvalue,
